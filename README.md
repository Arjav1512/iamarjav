# My Corner of the Internet (iamarjav.me)

This is my personal portfolio - a small piece of the web I built to show who I am, what I have worked on and what I care about.

Hey, 👋
I am Arjav. I am a CS student who builds things, gets distracted by human psychology, and occasionally co-founds things. This site is where all of that lives.

I don't have many hobbies outside of building. I am not athletic, bad at singing, don't drink and can't dance. Building is genuinely the only thing I am good at. I want to be a part of taking something from 0 → 1 or 1 → 100. That's the goal. Everything else is just noise I am trying to tune out.

---

If you want to poke around the code or spin it up locally:
```bash
pnpm install
pnpm dev
```

All the content - the bio, projects, experience, everything - lives in one place: `data/content.ts`. That was kind of the point. Update one file, and the whole site reflects it.

Built with ❤️ and a lot of tweaking at odd hours.
